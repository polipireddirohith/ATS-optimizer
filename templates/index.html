How Scoring Works

Our algorithm analyzes your resume across 4 critical pillars: Keyword Match, Skill Recalling, Experience Alignment, and Formatting. We use weighted vectors to ensure that mandatory job requirements carry the most impact.

  • Keywords (40%): Hard skills and domain-specific terminology.
  • Skills (25%): Categorized technical and soft competencies.
  • Experience (15%): Alignment with role seniority and responsibilities.
  • Formatting (10%): ATS-readability and document structure.
